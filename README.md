# ProtonESP

![ProtonESP](assets/PROTONEYES.jpeg)

ProtonESP is a low-cost AI robot project created for students and technology enthusiasts. ProtonESP is a compact and minimalist desktop robot designed to fit into the daily lives of students like me and create opportunities for people interested in engineering, programming, and artificial intelligence as a vision for future opportunities.


## Features
- OLED animations
- Voice interaction
- ESP32-S3 and some boards too
- AI assistant integration
- 3D printed body and models

## Hardware
- ESP32-S3
- OLED 128x64
- INMP441 microphone
- MAX98357 3W
- Small oval 8Ω speaker
- Small protoboard

## Goals
The purpose of ProtonESP is to offer low production cost while making both electronic components and learning more accessible. The project is designed to help people easily understand how every part works, including the code, artificial intelligence, and 3D model. For those who want to explore further, this simple little robot can become the beginning of much bigger projects and future innovations.
